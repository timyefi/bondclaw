# 订阅与案例政策

## Goal

Turn BondClaw from a pure tool into a lightweight subscription surface.

## Rules

- Prefer RSS, Atom, or JSON feeds.
- Poll locally and cache locally.
- Deduplicate before notifying.
- Tag content by topic and role.
- Keep a small manifest that describes source groups and notification order.
- Keep a case library that links feed topics to prompt workflows.
- Keep the starter case library at 20 cards until a new expansion wave is approved.
- Keep push optional and lightweight.
- Do not require a heavy backend for basic functionality.

## Notification Order

1. Local cache
2. Desktop notification
3. Optional website push
